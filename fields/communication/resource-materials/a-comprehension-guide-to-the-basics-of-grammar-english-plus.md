### Preface

&emsp;&emsp; Mastery on the basics of grammar is one of the tools in enhancing one's communicative skills. It is a guide to an individual in constructing correct sentences and paragraphs both in written and oral aspects. Almost all activities in school, at work, at home or anywhere includes relaying of information, expressing ideas and feelings, transmitting all kinds of messages and writing correspondence. All of these require correctness in grammar to fully understand the meaning of messages we receive; if these messages are not writen using correct grammar, then miscommunication will take place and will eventually lead to wrong information or feedback. Thus, it is necessary to master the basics of grammar to facilitate communicative types of activities.

&emsp;&emsp; This book gives you a comprehensive discussion of the rules of grammar and exercises that will test your mastery on the rules.
