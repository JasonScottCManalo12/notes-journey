1. What does it mean to rejoice?
     - Obligation Mindset
     - Philippians 4:4
2. Why is it sometimes difficult to rejoice?
     - We do not have an authentic relationship with God
     - We confuse joy and happiness
     - We try to manufacture joy
     - Our perspective is too limited
     - Isaiah 61:10
     - 1 Thessalonians 5:16
     - Philippians 4:4
3. What are the practical ways by which we enjoy God's presence?
     - Stay close to the source of joy
     - Remind yourself always to rejoice
     - Meditate on the goodness of God
